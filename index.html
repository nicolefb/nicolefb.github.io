My recent adventures!

Zakynthos, Greece

Zakynthos is one of the beautiful Greek islands in the Ionian Sea with plenty to do there! According to Greek mythology, the island is named after Zacynthus, the son of Dardanus, and the grandson of Zeus and Helectra.

My favourite part of the trip was going on a glass-bottom boat to go turtle-spotting, and getting to see the Caretta-Caretta turtles. On the same boat trip, we also got to visit Marithonisi, known as turtle island. Then, we visited the Keri Keri caves, which was an amazing experience! There's so much to do, with a famous nightlife scene, gorgeous wildlife, fun waterparks, authentic Greek food, and so much more!

Madeira, Portugal

Madeira is an island belonging to Portugal, situated in the northwest coast of Africa. It is a volcanic island with pebble beaches, famous for its wine.

My favourite part of the trip was going on a day trip to visit Funchal, which is Madeira's capital. It is like a town, and is quite a contrast to the relaxed, quiet, calm setting of where I stayed on the east coast. Due to its location, Madeira is perfect to go whale watching on a boat, and you can even swim with dolphins! It is also known for its nature, and if this is what you'd like to explore most, a hiking trip is perfect for you!

Barcelona, Spain

Barcelona is a city in Spain, famous for its arts and architecture.

A must see in Barcelona is the Basílicia de la Sagrada Familia, which is pictured above. The architecture is stunning, and the inside is even more beautiful. Another architectural masterpiece is Casa Batlló designed by Antoni Gaudí, and being able to walk through the building with a guide is such a fun experience! There are also many art tours, including the graffiti murals, and the Picasso Museum.
